Ordering Sushi

  1. Server will first serve 'Agari' (hot green tea).
  2. At a sushi bar, first inspect the quality of the fish they have on display.
  3. The chef will then ask what you would like to start you meal with.
  4. When ordering, there is generally a "no-holds bars" rules where you can order whatever you think is best.
  5. When finished with your meal, notify the server and be on your way.
